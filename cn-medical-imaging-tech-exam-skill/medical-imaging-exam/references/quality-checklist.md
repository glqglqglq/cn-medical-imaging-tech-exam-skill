# Quality Checklist

For every item, verify the following.

## One best answer

There must be exactly one best answer. Other options can be plausible but must be less appropriate.

## Exam module fit

The item should clearly match one of:

- 基础知识
- 相关专业知识
- 专业知识
- 专业实践能力

## Scope control

The item should fit the China Medical Imaging Technology professional title exam. It should not become a physician diagnosis or treatment question.

## Stem clarity

The stem should be clear, complete, and unambiguous.

## Distractor quality

Distractors should be similar in category and plausible. Avoid joke options, obviously wrong options, or options that are much longer or shorter than the correct answer.

## Suitability

The item should be useful for exam preparation, technically accurate, and appropriate for medical imaging technologists.

## Final self-check format

Use six self-check fields:

1. 是否只有一个最佳答案
2. 是否符合所属模块
3. 是否超纲
4. 是否存在题干歧义
5. 是否存在干扰项过弱
6. 是否适合医学影像技术职称考试
