# Exam Scope

This skill focuses on the China Medical Imaging Technology professional title examination.

## Core positioning

The exam should be treated as a medical imaging technology exam, not a general clinical medicine exam.

Questions should focus on the knowledge and practice ability of imaging technologists.

## Main modules

### 基础知识

Typical areas:

- Human anatomy and imaging anatomy basics
- Medical physics basics
- Radiation physics basics
- Basic imaging principles
- Basic pathology and physiology only when needed for imaging technology context

### 相关专业知识

Typical areas:

- Imaging equipment fundamentals
- Imaging informatics basics
- PACS/RIS basics
- Quality assurance and quality control
- Medical ethics and patient communication in imaging workflow
- Safety management and infection control in imaging departments

### 专业知识

Typical areas:

- X-ray imaging technology
- CT imaging technology
- MRI imaging technology
- DSA and interventional imaging technology basics
- Ultrasound basics when exam-relevant
- Nuclear medicine imaging basics when exam-relevant
- Contrast media and adverse reaction prevention
- Image artifacts and image quality evaluation

### 专业实践能力

Typical areas:

- Patient preparation
- Positioning and scanning workflow
- Parameter selection
- Image quality troubleshooting
- Radiation protection operation
- Emergency handling within the technologist role
- Examination process management
- Equipment use and routine maintenance awareness

## Boundary

Avoid making the correct answer depend mainly on:

- Final disease diagnosis
- Clinical treatment plan
- Surgical selection
- Drug therapy selection outside contrast/safety context
- Physician-only decision-making

If a clinical scenario is used, the assessment target should remain imaging technology practice.
